A Little About Me

A Little About Me.

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Ahmad Ansari, a visual artist specializing in photography, videography, and motion graphics. With a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Studio Art from the University of Wyoming, I have spent over a decade capturing compelling stories through images and films. My expertise spans various photography genres—including portrait, documentary, architectural, fashion, and aerial photography—along with professional video production for marketing, events, and social media. As a Video Producer at the University of Wyoming’s Center for Student Involvement & Leadership, I have created dynamic content that enhances engagement, earning recognition such as the ACUI "Best Video and Animation" award in 2024. My skills also extend to motion graphics, animation, and aerial cinematography, allowing me to craft visually striking narratives across multiple mediums. Thank you for visiting my portfolio—I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I enjoy creating it!

My Services

Photography

My photography spans a diverse range of styles, including portrait, documentary, architectural, landscape, fashion, wedding, and aerial photography. With a keen eye for composition and lighting, I strive to capture compelling images that tell stories and evoke emotions. My experience extends to studio and outdoor settings, where I experiment with different techniques to highlight the unique character of my subjects. Whether it's capturing grand architectural structures, the intimacy of a portrait, or the vast beauty of landscapes from above, I use photography as a medium to explore visual narratives with depth and meaning.

Videography

In videography, I specialize in producing dynamic content across various genres, including documentary, commercial, sports, event coverage, and promotional videos. My expertise includes cinematography, aerial videography using DJI drones, motion graphics, and visual effects, allowing me to create immersive storytelling experiences. I have worked extensively in video production, from conceptualizing and storyboarding to filming and post-production editing using Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects. My work also involves creating engaging marketing content, staff interviews, and event highlight videos that enhance audience engagement and brand storytelling. Through videography, I aim to craft visually compelling narratives that leave a lasting impact.
